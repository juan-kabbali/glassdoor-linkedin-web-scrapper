      • In a few words


      As a Senior Data Analyst, you will be at the core of Contentsquare’s value creation and innovation. You will be working closely with our UX/UI Designers and Customer Success Managers on a variety of data analysis projects and you will also contribute to Product and AI development with our R&D team.

       

      In the Data Analyst team, our motto is “Keep Creating”. We ask every team member to constantly suggest ways to improve and challenge the status quo, in whichever way possible. Constant innovation is a key component of our DNA.

       

      The role of a Data Analyst is a hybrid one, mixing two very different sets of skills:

       

      • Technical skills: Statistics, mathematical knowledge, advanced Excel-like tool knowledge, and data crunching ‘Big Data’ databases in SQL.
      • Consulting skills: The consulting skillset is harder to master, but involves being able to listen, understand and convince key decision-makers on the client side. Starting from raw data extraction, a Data Analyst will aim at transforming data into an appealing story, with simple and precise Data Visualisations, in an efficient and timely manner.


      On a typical day you might:

      • Conduct website audits along with deep analysis for our clients in collaboration with the Customer Success Managers, UX Designers and UI Designers.
      • Constantly suggest ways to improve our methodology or its application, and deliver projects based on these suggestions.
      • Work in collaboration with “the Lab” and the R&D team on AI development projects.


      What you'll need to succeed:

      • 2+ years experience in working with numbers and data (e.g. databases, Excel, statistics, data visualization)
      • You are fluent in French & English (C1 level at least)
      • Experience / skill in one of the following programming languages: SQL, Elastic Search, Python, R, or other data exploration language. If not, strong, evidence-based interest in learning technical skills.
      • Knowledge of the digital ecosystem: business models, analytics, acquisition, ecommerce strategies.
      • You are extremely motivated and excited to join Contentsquare adventure. You feel like working in a very ambitious team, within a rapidly growing company, matches your career goals.
      • You strongly believe that continuous learning is a requirement for your next job. You are excited by innovation and quick change.
      • You are a proactive team-player.


      Good to know:

      • Start date: As soon as you're available
      • Contract: CDI
      • Location: We are located in the center of Paris, near la Madeleine


      As well as the opportunity to have a tangible impact on the success of the global business here’s what else we offer:

      • Plenty of opportunities for training and development
      • Flexible approach to working hours and holidays
      • Attractive people care approach with good health insurance
      • Fun office culture and strong team spirit, with beers every Friday and quarterly parties
      • Regular breakfasts and fruit in the office each week
      • 25 days PTO + 10 days RTT
      • Many benefits via our CSE such as reductions on gym membership and leisure activities


      Does this sound like it was written for you? Excellent! Please apply and let’s explore this together.
