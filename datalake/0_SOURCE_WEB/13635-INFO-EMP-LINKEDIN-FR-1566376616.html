They require a Data Scientist to help them use analytics to answer their client's key business questions e.g. customer lifetime value, churn, loyalty etc...

                          Responsibilities:
                          • Help with customer insights
                          • Create custom, production-ready machine learning models
                          • Drive projects independently
                          • Asist the Lead Data Scientist and CDO on delivery of the Data Roadmap
                          • Collaborate with other teams to solve business problems with data-driven decisions

                          Key Skills/Requirements:
                          • At least 2 years experience as a Data Scientist
                          • Experience of working in Marketing and Advertising or Consumer Goods
                          • Have an advanced degree in machine learning, statistics, computer science
                          • Experience with Phyton, Spark or PySpark
                          • Are passionate about building prototypes and putting your work into a production environment
                          • Experience of modelling, data management, information systems and related software (e.g. SQL, SAS, R, Tableau, Excel/VBA, Qlik)
